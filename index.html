Set token in title bar

This will allow you to set the token name in your title bar.

How to setup:

  • Make sure your bookmarks bar is showing your browser.
  • Drag the "Set Title" link below into your bookmarks bar.
  • Once you have navigated to an EtherDelta page, click the bookmark and watch the title change.
